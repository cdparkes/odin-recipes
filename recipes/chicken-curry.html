Chicken Curry

A lovely and creamy Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 500g Chicken breast
  • 3 Tbsp Curry paste
  • 200ml Cream
  • 250ml pasturized Tomatoes
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook rice following the instructions on the package.
  2. Finely dice onion and cut chicken into bite size pieces.
  3. Fry off onion and chicken in a hot frying pan.
  4. When chicken is brown add curry paste, cream and tomatoes.
  5. Cook for about 10 - 15 minutes or till chicken is cooked.
  6. Serve with rice and nan bread
