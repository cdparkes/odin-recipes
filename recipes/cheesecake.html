Cheesecake

A beautiful Baked German Cheesecake

Absolutly lovely Baked Cheesecake that is a favorite at friends and family of mine.

Super simple and low hassle recipe. Done in about 2 hours.

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 125g Butter of Margarine (soft)
  • 125g Sugar
  • 250g Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Egg

For the filling

  • 500g quark
  • 1 Egg
  • 150g Sugar
  • 1 satchel vanilla sugar
  • 1 stachel vanilla custard powder
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Mix together butter, sugar, flour, baking powder, salt and egg to a firm dough.
  2. Place in round baking pan and press onto bottom and sides. Sides should be 3 - 4cm high.
  3. Mix together quark, egg, sugar, vanilla sugar, vanilla custard powder, vegetable oil and milk and pour into baking pan
  4. Place in pre heated oven at 180Â°C for 1 hour
